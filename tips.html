Tips on Reducing Electricity Usage

  • Turn off any unnecessary electronics that is not currently
  • Turning off light bulb at least four hours a day.
  • Use table lamp rather than ceiling lights in work and hobby areas.
  • Use microwave four times a week rather than an oven.
  • Unplug unused electronics.
  • Only use your washing machine when you have full load.
  • Use cold water to wash your clothes.
